About Me

I'm a 2nd-year Biomedical Engineering student with a growing passion for software development. I started coding this year and have been building consistently ever since — learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript from scratch while juggling a full engineering curriculum.

What drives me is the intersection of the two fields. Biomedical Engineering teaches me how the human body works as a system; software gives me the tools to build things that interact with it. My goal is to work on health tech and medical software — products that make a real difference in how people are diagnosed, monitored, and treated.

I'm still early in this journey, but I show up every day, build things, and keep improving.
